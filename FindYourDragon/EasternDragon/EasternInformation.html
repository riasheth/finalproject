Eastern Dragon Facts and Information

The Eastern Dragon is literally a dog. It will trail around you, begging for food and attention until it gets what it wants. However, it will also defend you at all costs, treating you like it's holy overlord. Nevertheless, it may be too attentive at times. It is easy to tame, and easy to get along with. Among all the dragons, the Eastern Dragon is the most amiable, and may novices choose the Eastern over all other dragon types. Appearance: Their green scales are hard once they're out of the egg. Though their scales aren't as rough as the Southern's, they have relatively rough scales. They've a wyvern, with no front legs but they have hind legs and a pair of wings. Diet: Easterns eat meat and only meat. They are strictly carniverous.
