The Western Dragon is a typical dragon with 4 legs and a pair of wings. They are carnivores with a diet of meat and seafood. Their size is pretty large. Also, they are big attention seekers and require a lot of attention. They are very loyal, along with being fire-breathers. They also have smooth scales. These are really great dragons to keep.
